About Us

Filipinos are recognized for their outgoing, cheerful, and hospitable personalities. They are generally polite and helpful and they'll make you feel right at home. Philippine cuisine bears the indelible mark of Spanish flavors blended seamlessly with native flavors and spices. Moreover, it has piqued the interest of foodies all over the world. But other than the people and their food, the Philippines is a tropical paradise in the Pacific Ocean made up of 7,107 islands that offer a natural playground for your inner explorer. Collectively, its landscapes feature an array of natural wonders such as the beautiful beaches of Boracay and Palawan.
