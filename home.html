~ A Beginner's Guide To The Philippines

"It's more fun in the Philippines!"

Filipinos are recognized for their outgoing, cheerful, and hospitable personalities. They are generally polite and helpful and they'll make you feel right at home. Philippine cuisine bears the indelible mark of Spanish flavors blended seamlessly with native flavors and spices. Moreover, it has piqued the interest of foodies all over the world.

But other than the people and their food, the Philippines is a tropical paradise in the Pacific Ocean made up of 7,107 islands that offer a natural playground for your inner explorer. Collectively, its landscapes feature an array of natural wonders such as the beautiful beaches of Boracay and Palawan.

But there’s more to the Philippines than just being an idyllic tropical destination for island-hopping getaways and beach adventures. It is also home to world-renowned natural wonders like volcanoes, underwater rivers, rice terraces, limestone caves, and incredible diving spots rich in biodiversity, colorful public transportation, unique cuisine, vibrant festivals that showcase its colorful culture, and friendly locals regarded as some of the happiest in the world.

Since the country is so spread out, a trip to the Philippines requires careful planning so that you can connect with the proper guides and find the most convenient lodging as a base for your favorite activities.

As a result, we've narrowed down some of the best tourist attractions in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao to help you plan your trip.
